Quaker Springs Volunteer Fire Department meetings are held the first Monday of each month at the Quaker Springs Fire Department Station #1, 107 Blodgett Road, Schuylerville, NY. 12871.

Exceptions: the September meeting is held the first Tuesday and the December meeting is held the first Thursday.

Line Officers meet at at Station 1 at 6:00pm the last Monday of each month.
